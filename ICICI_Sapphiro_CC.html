ICICI Sapphiro Credit Card – Comprehensive Rewards Guide

Executive Summary

The ICICI Sapphiro Credit Card provides a multifaceted reward ecosystem for frequent spenders and travelers. This guide outlines earning structures, redemption strategies, and optimization techniques grounded in ICICI’s policy and analysis.

Reward Point Accrual and Valuation

  • 2 RPs per ₹100 for domestic spends (0.5%)
  • 4 RPs per ₹100 for international spends (1%)
  • 1 RP per ₹100 for utilities, insurance, and fuel (0.25%)

Expiry Policy

Points expire after 36 months. Redeem older points first.

Redemption Options

1. Statement Credit

  • 4 RPs = ₹1
  • Fee: ₹116.82 per redemption
  • Net for 10,000 RPs: ₹2,383.18

2. E-Vouchers

  • Options: Amazon, Flipkart, Tata CLiQ, Uber
  • 1 RP = ₹0.25 (No fees)
  • Delivery: 5–10 business days via email

3. Product Catalog

4. Air Miles

  • 5 RPs = 1 InterMile
  • Value: ₹0.20–₹0.25 per RP
  • Requires linking InterMiles account

Redemption Process

Via Net Banking

  1. Login at icicibank.com
  2. Navigate to Rewards section
  3. Select and redeem e-vouchers, products, or travel

Via iMobile App

  1. Open App → "Rewards" → "Redeem Points"
  2. Choose a category and confirm

For Statement Credit

  1. Call customer care: 1860-258-5000
  2. Request points to be credited as statement balance

Optimization Strategies

  • Redeem ≥50,000 points at once to reduce fee impact
  • Align redemptions post milestone spends for bonuses
  • Set reminders for 36-month point expiry
  • Mix e-vouchers (max value) with statement credits (liquidity)

Limitations

  • No points for fuel, wallets, EMIs, or cash advances
  • Foreign currency markup: 3.5%
  • Annual fee: ₹3,500 (waived on ₹6L spend)

Insurance Coverage

  • Air Accident: ₹3 crore
  • Baggage Delay/Loss: ₹84,000
  • Eligibility: Use card 1x in 60 days before travel

Conclusion

The ICICI Sapphiro Credit Card can deliver strong returns when used strategically. Combine high-value redemptions like e-vouchers with milestone alignment and large redemptions for optimal value.
